Project Summary

Topic and Purpose of the Site

My topic for this project is to create a website for users to learn how to bake simple pastries at home. I decided to use this topic because I commonly bake at home and love to eat all sorts of pastries.

I hope that this website can inspire others to enjoy the excitement of creating a delicious cake, cookie, or loaf of bread from their own hands and share it with their friends. All in all, the purpose of my website is to spread the joy I personally find in baking through my site that offers clear, easy-to-follow instructions for cookies, cakes, bread, and other treats.

Features Implemented

  • Homepage & Recipe page: My homepage is seperate from my recipes page because the homepage acts as an introduction for my page and what it does, including what types of pastries there are recipes for and who I am, as the creater of the site. The recipes page provides links to the exact recipes that my website provides.
  • Buttons: There are buttons on the hopepage that guide the user towards the recipes page and the contact page. These buttons are relative to the information that it is below, making it convenient for the user to access other pages. In addition, buttons are also available on the bottom of all the specific recipe pages, allowing the user to return to the main recipe page without scrolling to the navigation bar at the top, increasing convenience.
  • Varioius Recipe Choices: There are pages for 4 recipes on my website. This makes the website more professional and useful for users.
  • Tips: The tips page is provide users with solutions to common issues that may arise while baking. This page is similar to FAQ pages on other websites that try to predict certain problems users may encounter while following the procedures on the website.
  • Contact Form: I have created a Contact Form to adhere to our project requirements and to provide users with ways to contact me when they have any questions, concerns, or wish to collaborate with my website.
