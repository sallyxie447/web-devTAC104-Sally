Banana Bread Recipe

This beginner-friendly banana bread is soft, moist, and packed with real banana flavor. It’s the perfect recipe for using up ripe bananas and makes a comforting treat for breakfast, snacks, or anytime you want something homemade and cozy.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

Steps to Make Banana Bread:

  1. Preheat the oven

    Set your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a loaf pan or line it with parchment paper.

  2. Mash the bananas

    In a large bowl, mash 3 ripe bananas until smooth.

  3. Mix in the wet ingredients

    Add ½ cup melted butter, ¾ cup sugar (or less for a less sweet loaf), 1 large egg, 1 tsp vanilla extract

    Stir until combined.

  4. Add the dry ingredients

    Mix in: 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour, 1 tsp baking soda, ½ tsp salt

    Gently fold the mixture until just combined (don’t overmix!).

  5. Pour into the loaf pan

    Spread the batter evenly.

  6. Bake

    Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

  7. Cool & serve

    Let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack. Slice and enjoy!
