Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft, chewy, and packed with chocolate chips, these cookies are an easy, beginner-friendly treat. Perfect for satisfying sweet cravings, gifting to friends, or enjoying a little homemade comfort anytime.

Ingredients:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 ½ cups granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps to Make Chocolate Chip Cookies:

  1. Preheat the oven

    Preheat to 375°F (190°C).

  2. Mix dry ingredients

    In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Cream the butter and sugars

    In a large bowl, beat the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add eggs and vanilla

    Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add vanilla.

  5. Combine wet and dry

    Gradually mix the dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined.

  6. Fold in chocolate chips

    Stir in the chocolate chips.

  7. Scoop dough

    Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  8. Bake

    Bake for 9–11 minutes, until the edges are golden brown.

  9. Cool

    Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.

    Enjoy the delicious treat!
