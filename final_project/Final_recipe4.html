Vanilla Cupcakes

Deliciously soft, moist, and packed with classic vanilla flavor, these cupcakes are simple to bake at home. Perfect for parties, cozy afternoons, or whenever you crave a sweet treat that’s homemade and comforting.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • ½ cup whole milk
  • your desired amount of frosting

Steps to Make New York Cheesecake:

  1. Preheat the oven

    Preheat to 350°F (175°C) and line a 12-cup muffin pan with cupcake liners.

  2. Mix dry ingredients

    In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Set aside.

  3. Cream butter and sugar

    In a large bowl, beat the softened butter and sugar until light and fluffy.

  4. Add eggs and vanilla

    Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add vanilla extract.

  5. Combine wet and dry ingredients

    Gradually add the dry ingredients, alternating with milk, beginning and ending with dry ingredients. Mix until just combined.

    Pour hot water into the roasting pan until it reaches halfway up the sides of the springform pan.

  6. Fill cupcake liners

    Divide the batter evenly among the 12 cupcake liners, filling each about 2/3 full.

  7. Bake

    Bake for 18–22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

  8. Cool

    Let cupcakes cool in the pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

  9. Frost and serve

    Frost with your choice of frosting once cooled.
