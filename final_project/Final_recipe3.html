Cheesecake

This classic New York cheesecake is rich, creamy, and perfectly smooth. Easy to make at home, it’s ideal for special occasions, dessert tables, or whenever you want a luxurious, homemade treat.

Ingredients:

Crust

  • 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted

Filling

  • 4 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 ¼ cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 4 large eggs

Optional

  • Fresh Berry Jam
  • Whipping cream

Steps to Make New York Cheesecake:

  1. Preheat the oven

    Preheat to 325°F (163°C).

  2. Prepare the crust

    Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and melted butter until combined.

    Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.

    Bake for 8–10 minutes, then let it cool.

  3. Mix the filling

    Beat the softened cream cheese until smooth and creamy.

    Add the sugar and continue mixing until well combined.

    Add sour cream and vanilla; mix until smooth.

  4. Add eggs

    Add the eggs one at a time, mixing on low speed just until incorporated (do not overmix).

  5. Set up a water bath

    Place the springform pan inside a large roasting pan.

    Pour hot water into the roasting pan until it reaches halfway up the sides of the springform pan.

  6. Bake the cheesecake

    Bake for 60–75 minutes, until the edges are set and the center still has a slight jiggle.

  7. Cool gradually

    Remove from the water bath and cool to room temperature.

    Refrigerate for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.

  8. Add any additions

    spread some jam or whipping cream on the top.

  9. Serve

    Run a knife around the sides before releasing the springform ring. Share and Enjoy!
