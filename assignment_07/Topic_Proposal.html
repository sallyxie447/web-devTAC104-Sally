Sally's Bakery: Home Baking for Beginners

Sally's Bakery is a warm, welcoming guide designed to help new bakers discover the joy of creating homemade treats. From simple recipes to essential tips, this site makes learning to bake easy, fun, and rewarding.

Audience:

This website is designed for beginner and casual bakers — especially students, young adults, and anyone looking to start baking at home without complicated techniques or equipment.

Design & Style

The design will be warm, cozy, and minimalist, using soft pastel colors and rounded edges to create a welcoming atmosphere that reflects the joy of home baking.

Color Palette:

  • Primary: Cream (#FFF8F0)
  • Secondary: Soft Pink (#F7B7A3)
  • Accent: Warm Brown (#A7745B)
  • Text: Dark Brown (#4B3B33)

Inspirations:

  • My Baking Addiction
  • Baking the Goods
  • Broma

Scope:

The website will include four pages:

  1. Home — Introduction to Bake & Bloom with an overview video.
  2. Recipes — A gallery of beginner-friendly recipes with photos and short descriptions.
  3. Tips — Baking advice and troubleshooting for common mistakes.
  4. Contact — A form for visitors to share feedback or recipe ideas (with confirmation page).

Each page will have navigation, header, and footer sections.
