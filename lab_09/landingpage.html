Concert Video

Keshi is a singer-songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist known for his smooth blend of R&B, lo-fi, and pop sounds. I have been a huge fan of his since hearing his song, skeletons, which was approximately 4 years ago.

The video below captures a moment from the Keshi concert I attended, one of the most unforgettable nights I’ve experienced. The atmosphere was electric — the crowd sang every lyric, and Keshi’s voice sounded even better live than on recordings. What stood out to me most was how personal and emotional his performance felt, especially during the slower songs. You could feel the connection between him and the audience through every word and melody. Watching this video always reminds me of the power of music to bring people together and create lasting memories.
