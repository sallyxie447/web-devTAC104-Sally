Banana Bread Recipe

This beginner-friendly banana bread is soft, moist, and packed with real banana flavor. It’s the perfect recipe for using up ripe bananas and makes a comforting treat for breakfast, snacks, or anytime you want something homemade and cozy.

Ingredients:

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
